Universal Credits

Oracle Universal Credits provide a predictable, consistent way to pay for any Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) service in any region,* including future releases of cloud services. Credits are debited as you use OCI services over the course of a year, giving you the flexibility to ramp up your workloads.

The benefits of Universal Credits span all OCI regions

Budget for predictable spend

Customers commit to an annual spend of Universal Credits that can be used at any time for any OCI services. This helps alleviate budget surprises and smooth expenditures.

Choose your services

Universal Credits can be used toward any eligible OCI service in any OCI region.* Customers can change service usage at any time, offering flexibility without being locked in.

Get discounted prices

Customers purchasing under the Universal Credit model can receive discounts on OCI service prices, including new services when they become available.

Using Universal Credits

The Universal Credit model is the most flexible buying and consumption model for Oracle Cloud Infrastructure services.

There are two ways to purchase OCI services from Oracle: (i) Pay As You Go (PAYG) and (ii) the Universal Credit annual commitment model.

Credits can be applied to eligible services at any time in any OCI region.*

Actual OCI service usage is debited from the customer's contractual amount of Universal Credits. Unused credits expire after the contract term ends.

To learn more, review the Oracle PaaS and IaaS Universal Credits Service Descriptions.
